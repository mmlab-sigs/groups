🔬Research

With the rapid rise of large-scale pretrained models and autonomous intelligence, AI is increasingly moving from the digital realm into the physical world, catalyzing a new industrial transformation centered on industrial foundation models and embodied industrial intelligence.

  • Autonomous multimedia intelligence
  • Distributed machine learning
  • Industrial foundation models
  • Embodied industrial intelligence

👥Research Groups

  • Efficient Deep Learning & Embodiment→ Model compression, efficient neural architectures, and deploying deep learning on resource-constrained embodied systems for real-time robotic perception and control.
  • Agentic & Embodied Large Model Training→ Training and accelerating large vision-language-action models for agentic decision-making, with focus on scalable distributed training and embodied AI deployment.
  • Self-Evolving Embodied Agents→ Autonomous agents that continuously self-improve through interaction with physical environments, leveraging world models and reinforcement learning.
